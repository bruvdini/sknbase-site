FAQ

Questions, answered

When does SKNBASE launch?

We're launching on the App Store and Google Play in August 2026. The app is in active beta right now — email us for early access.

Is this medical advice?

No. SKNBASE gives general, cosmetic skincare guidance based on visible, non-medical observations. For persistent acne, severe pigmentation, or any medical concern, please see a licensed dermatologist.

Which cities does it work for?

Any Nigerian city. The engine scores every product for your local climate — humid coastal cities like Lagos and Port Harcourt get different texture recommendations than drier cities like Abuja and Kano.

Do you sell the products?

No — and that keeps our recommendations honest. Every product links out to trusted Nigerian retailers with live prices, so you buy directly from stores that stock it.

What happens to my face scan photos?

They're stored privately and encrypted, visible only to your account, and used solely to generate your analysis and progress tracking. You can permanently delete your account and all your data in-app at any time.

How much does it cost?

The core experience is free, including one full skin analysis a month. Premium is ₦4,000/month and unlocks unlimited analyses, your complete routine, face scans, and full progress tracking.
